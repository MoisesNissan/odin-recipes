Tater Tot

This Tater Tot casserole is a quick and easy dinner that everyone will love. Just four basic ingredients come together for this comforting dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • mushroom soup
  • salt and ground black pepper
  • package frozen tater tots

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  3. Transfer beef mixture to a 9x13-inch baking dish
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until tater tots are golden brown
